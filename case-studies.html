Primary Case Study

Venture Capital

Early-stage venture fund · 2-person investment team · VC / Financial services

The Challenge

Venture Capital screens hundreds of inbound deals per year with a two-person team. Before AI Deploy, every deal required manual triage — reading pitch decks, researching founders, assessing thesis fit, and writing screening notes. The team was spending the majority of their time on deal administration instead of the relationship-building and due diligence that actually drives returns.

Email was a warzone. Founder intros, LP correspondence, legal documents, and noise all sat in the same inbox. Meeting notes were rough and action items got lost. Portfolio monitoring was equally manual — tracking milestones, preparing LP updates, and maintaining CRM records consumed hours every week.

The Solution

Phase 1 — Audit + Free Starter Pack ($999 one-off)

AI Deploy audited Venture Capital's entire investment workflow, from deal sourcing through to LP reporting. The audit scored each workflow on a friction-automation matrix and delivered a ranked roadmap.

In the same week, the Free Starter Pack was deployed:

  • Email Triage — Gmail + Gemini configured to sort the inbox: founder intros, LP comms, legal docs, noise. Auto-draft responses for standard inbound.
  • Meeting Notes — Zoom AI Companion configured for founder calls, LP meetings, and board meetings. Structured notes with action items appear in Notion automatically.
  • Document Drafter — Prompt templates in Google Docs + Gemini for LP updates, follow-up emails, reference check templates.
  • Context-Aware Assistant — Chat interface connected to Notion, Gmail, and Drive.

Phase 2 — Deal Flow Booster Pack ($999/month)

  • Deal Quick Screen — 30-second triage on every inbound deal. Checks thesis fit, kill-shot gates, instant PASS/PROCEED verdict.
  • Deal Screener — Full scoring engine: 6-dimension investment rubric, 8-dimension founder rubric, interactive HTML report with ADVANCE / MONITOR / PASS verdict.
  • Deck Analyser — Processes pitch decks, extracts key metrics, flags inconsistencies, generates structured summaries.
  • Inbound Sorter — Triages incoming emails and LinkedIn intros by thesis fit.
