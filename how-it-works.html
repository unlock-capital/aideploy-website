The Process

Four phases. One outcome: AI that works.

1

Audit + Free Starter Pack

~1 week · $999 one-off

We start with a conversation. You tell us about your business, your team, and the workflows that eat the most time. Then we go deep — mapping your operations, scoring each workflow for automation potential, and delivering a ranked roadmap with ROI projections.

But you don't wait for the roadmap to see value. In the same week, we configure three free automations on your existing platforms — email triage, meeting notes, and document drafting — plus a Context-Aware Assistant connected to your business data. Free for 12 months.

What you bring

Your knowledge of your own operations. Access to your team for 3–4 working sessions.

What you leave with

A prioritised automation blueprint, ROI projections, three working automations, and a clear recommendation on which booster packs to deploy first.

2

Booster Pack Deploy

1–2 weeks per pack · $999/month per pack

We take the top opportunities from your audit and build purpose-built AI skills — 3 to 5 per pack, tailored to your department and your workflows. Each skill is integrated into your existing tools, tested with your actual data, and your team is trained before we go live.

No new platforms to learn. No code for you to write. Your team keeps working the way they already do — just faster.

What you bring

Feedback during testing. Access to your tools for integration.

What you leave with

Working AI skills in production — not prototypes. Plus enablement sessions so your people are confident, not confused.

3

Pack Expansion

1–2 weeks per additional pack · $999/month each

Once your first pack is running and delivering results, expand into the next priority area. Most clients start with one pack and add as value is proven. Stack up to four packs for a full-suite deployment covering every function — max $3,996/month.

What you bring

Nothing beyond your first-pack experience — you know the process now.

What you leave with

Additional departments automated. Compounding time savings across your operation.

4

Managed Services

Ongoing · Included in every pack

We don't leave after deployment. Managed services means we monitor performance, fix issues, optimise skills based on real usage data, and handle new requests through your 24/7 kanban board. Every month, you receive an impact report showing exactly what your investment returned — hours saved, costs avoided, ROI delivered.

The Skill Factory — our autonomous development agent — processes most requests automatically. Submit a request on your kanban, and it gets triaged, built, tested, and deployed. Simple requests are handled same-day without human intervention.

What you bring

Nothing ongoing — we manage it.

What you leave with

Monthly proof that AI is working for your business. Skills that get better over time because we're still there, making them better.
